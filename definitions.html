Basic CRE Definitions

Loan Vehicles

  • Conventional Loans: Low-interest rates and long repayment periods.
  • SBA 7(a) Loans: Government-backed with favorable terms for small businesses.
  • CMBS Loans: Lower interest rates for strong credit borrowers.
  • Bridge Loans: Short-term funding for property improvements or quick acquisitions.

Investor Structures

  • General Partnerships: Equal management and shared liability.
  • Limited Partnerships: General partners manage, limited partners provide funding.

Return Metrics

  • Cash-on-Cash Return: Annual cash flow divided by initial investment.
  • Internal Rate of Return (IRR): Total return over the holding period.
  • Equity Multiple: Total return relative to equity invested.
